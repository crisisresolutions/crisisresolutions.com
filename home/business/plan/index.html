Executive Summary

Crisis Resolutions is a mission-driven organization that is dedicated to promoting good governance without biased judgments and through the practice of good ways. Our work is centered around eight approaches: personal, mental, practical, social, ethical, mutual, empirical, and vital. We offer several Membership Plans tailored to meet the needs of different people and organizations, and our budget is carefully planned to ensure responsible and effective distribution of our funds. Additionally, we prioritize upholding and complying with international standards of sustainability. Ultimately, we strive to empower individuals and professionals to invest in their own well-being and that of others, and we welcome all who share this vision to join us in our mission.

Business Description

Crisis Resolutions is an organization that provides access to essential services and goods, with a focus on personal growth. The organization’s unique Friendship Plans are tailored to people’s different needs and circumstances, providing resources and support for a fulfilling life. Crisis Resolutions is committed to sustainable development and long-term sustainability, with a flexible budget plan that ensures the organization can provide essential services to those who need them most.

Crisis Resolutions’ Good Ways model promotes Good Governance and gamification, encouraging members to contribute to positive change in their communities. The model is a comprehensive approach to personal and social growth, promoting kindness, supportiveness, forgiveness, responsibility, thoughtfulness, and respectfulness in all human interactions. It stands as the foundation of Crisis Resolutions’ Crisis Management System, which is compliant with international standards and guidelines, including the ISO 22301:2019 for business continuity management and the ISO 9001 for quality management.

The Crisis Management System utilizes training simulations based on the Good Ways Model for Self-Governance to resolve crises, allowing individuals to practice and apply Good Ways in a safe and controlled environment. By gamifying the learning process, Crisis Resolutions encourages individuals to continue learning and growing their skills, preparing them to deal with real-world situations. Knowledge architecture is used as a model for good governance, creating efficient and sustainable systems that benefit all stakeholders.

Crisis Resolutions is committed to promoting good practices and implementing the Good Ways model, empowering individuals to become better versions of themselves and positively impact their communities. Through its unique approach, Crisis Resolutions is well-positioned for growth and expansion, making it an attractive partner for organizations looking to improve their operations and achieve their goals.

Market Analysis

Crisis Resolutions operates in various markets, including the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the Governance Market. The organization’s focus on sustainable development aligns with the goals of the UN’s SDGs, which aim to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for all. By providing essential services and goods to people regardless of their financial situation, Crisis Resolutions contributes to the achievement of these goals.

In the Governance Market, Crisis Resolutions offers good governance services to organizations to help them improve their operations and achieve their goals. This includes providing training and support to help organizations improve their financial management, human resource management, and other areas of operation. The Governance Market is a growing market, with organizations increasingly recognizing the importance of good governance in achieving their objectives.

Crisis Resolutions also operates in other markets, including the Business and Finance Markets. Financially, the markets in which Crisis Resolutions operates are estimated to be worth billions of dollars globally. The Governance Market alone is projected to reach $90 billion by 2027, while the Business and Finance Markets are projected to reach $22.6 trillion by 2025. Crisis Resolutions’ unique approach through gamification and the Good Ways model differentiates them from others in the market, making them well-positioned for growth and expansion.

Overall, Crisis Resolutions’ focus on sustainable development, good governance, and ethical practices make them an attractive partner for organizations looking to improve their operations and achieve their goals. With the growing demand for sustainable and ethical practices, Crisis Resolutions is well-positioned for growth and expansion in the markets in which they operate.

Marketing and Sales Strategy

Our marketing and sales strategy is focused on promoting the benefits of practicing Good Ways and how it can positively impact personal and professional success. We will use targeted social media campaigns and email marketing to reach our target audience. We will also use speaking engagements and community events to promote our brand and services.

In addition, we will develop partnerships with other non-profit organizations that share our mission. We will leverage these partnerships to expand our reach and promote our services to a wider audience.

We will also offer referral incentives to our existing members, encouraging them to bring in new members and expand our community. Our sales strategy will be focused on providing excellent customer service and support, ensuring that our members have a positive experience with our organization.

To further enhance our marketing strategy, we will focus on Omni-Personal Marketing. This means that we will produce A.I. Enhanced Content, Products, and Services that offer Tailor-made Solutions to a wide variety of topics. Our marketing approach will be to focus on being relevant and through qualitative quantitative productions with the help of Artificial Intelligence.

Operations Plan

At Crisis Resolutions, we practice our Good Ways Operations model, which is designed to ensure that all of our operations are carried out in an ethical, sustainable, and effective manner. Our model is compliant with ISO and other relevant standards, and we are committed to continuously improving our operations to meet the highest standards of quality.

As we are an online organization, our operations plan is focused on utilizing digital tools and platforms to carry out our work. We use cloud-based software for our financial management, project management, and communication needs. We work remotely, and we have established clear communication protocols and workflows to ensure that everyone is working effectively and efficiently.

Our operations plan is designed to be flexible and adaptable, allowing us to respond to changes in the market or organization quickly. We regularly review our operations to identify areas for improvement and implement changes as needed.

In addition, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of data protection and privacy. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect our data and ensure that it is handled in a responsible and compliant manner.

Overall, our operations plan is designed to ensure that we can achieve our mission and provide essential services and goods to those who need them most. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality and ethics in all of our operations and continuously improving our processes to meet the evolving needs of our organization and the world around us.

People

We offer different types of memberships in the Crisis Resolutions organization. These memberships include Players, Partners, and Volunteering members. Members are also our Stuff, and we approach everyone as Friends.

Players are active members who participate in training, games, and events. They are provided with access to exclusive content, discounts, and promotions. This membership category is ideal for individuals who are passionate about the cause and want to actively contribute to the organization’s mission.

Partners, on the other hand, support the organization through sponsorships, donations, or collaborations. Members in this category receive access to networking opportunities, marketing exposure, and special events. This membership category is perfect for businesses and organizations that want to make a tangible impact on the community and support a good cause.

Volunteering is a membership category for those who want to contribute to the organization’s mission and goals through volunteering their time and skills. Members in this category have access to training, support, and recognition programs. The volunteering category is ideal for individuals who are passionate about the cause but may not have the financial resources to donate or the time to participate in events.

Overall, the Crisis Resolutions organization offers a range of membership options to cater to different interests and abilities. Whether you are interested in actively participating in events, supporting the organization through sponsorships and donations, or contributing your skills through volunteering, there is a membership category for you. The document also includes links to more detailed information about each membership category, making it easy for interested individuals to learn more and get involved.

Financial Plan

Our annual budget covers expenses such as payroll, products, and platforms, as well as setting aside a portion for taxes and other unexpected costs.

Our financial plan includes a balanced budget, where our expenditure does not exceed our income. We will generate income through private memberships, good governance services, and public programs. Our funds will be distributed as follows: 62% to people (payroll), 23% to projects (product), 9% to places (platforms), and 6% to protection (plus).

We plan for the long term, assessing how our decisions now will affect our funds in the future. Contingency plans ensure our operations remain unaffected by any changes in the market or organization. We also review our budget regularly to make sure it is meeting our organization’s goals and objectives.

Center of Crisis Resolutions: Financial Plan Under Calibration

We are actively calibrating our Financial Plan to ensure it adheres to best practices and allows us to launch our organization in the most efficient way possible. We appreciate your patience and understanding as we work to finalize this important aspect of our business.
