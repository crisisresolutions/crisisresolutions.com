About Time

ChronOS, short for Chronological Operating System, is a non-deterministic model. It does not promise benefits through strict practice, but rather through collective effort. We can cultivate and sustain its goals for wellbeing, but it does not provide solutions to our crises without personal effort.

How we do it?

  • We apply the Principle of Chirality in Time Management, which means that we Measure Time with our Hands as a Blueprint.
  • We adopt our Good Ways Model as a Natural and Proactive Practice of Good Governance for Crisis Resolutions.
  • We provide you with a Time Management Tool to help resolve and relieve crises. This tool introduces Predictable Variety, also known as Entropy, which is a critical principle for the existence of life in nature and a standard in computing for better performance and security.
